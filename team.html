Our Team

Prof. Anil K. Bhowmick

Head of Advisory Panel

Prof. Anil K. Bhowmick is a Professor of Eminence and former Head of Rubber Technology Centre and former Dean of Postgraduate Studies and former Dean (Sponsored Research & Industrial Consultancy), IIT Kharagpur. He was also a foreign students` advisor of the institute. He was previously associated with the University of Akron, Ohio, USA, London School of Polymer Technology, London and Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan.

Dr. Atul Thakur

Faculty In-Charge

Dr. Atul Thakur is Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna and broadly work in the area of mobile robotics His research interests include bio-inspired robotics, physics-aware planning of robotic systems, and application of robotics techniques for micro-manipulation of biological cells
