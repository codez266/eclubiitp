Entrepreneurs Week

Celebrating the spirit of Entrepreneurship


   Events

Entrepreneurship Club, IIT Patna organized its first "Entrepreneur's Week" over March 2-7, 2014 at the IIT Patna campus. The week-long event included guest lectures, workshops and panel discussions featuring eminent personalities from industry as well as academia. They elucidated the various stages of innovation and nuances involved in starting a business. The event was organised with an aim to ignite the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the students by spreading awareness on the risks and opportunities involved in starting one's own company.

  • Day 1 (March 2, 2014)
    Inaugural Guest Lecture by Padma Shri Dr. Anil K Gupta, Executive Vice Chair, National Innovation Foundation
  • Day 2 (March 3, 2014)
    Workshop on "Building Your First Prototype" by Mr. Anandghan Waghmare, IIT Patna alumni and creator of a Braille-based phone for the blind.
  • Day 3(March 4, 2014)
    Workshop titled "Introduction to Bitcoin - the new face of entrepreneurship" by Mr. Vikram Nikkam, Co-founder, Unocoin.
  • Day 4 (March 5, 2014)
    Late Night Lecture and Interaction Session with Mr. Arjun Malhotra, Co-founder, HCL and Co-founder, TechSpan.
  • Day 5 (March 6, 2014)
    Panel Discussion on "How to Bolster the Entrepreneurship Ecosystem in India?" and Website Launch
    Panel Members:
    • Mr. Arjun Malhotra (Co-founder, HCL and Co-founder, TechSpan)
    • Ms Padmaja Ruparel (Founder President, Indian Angel Network)
    • Mr. Anjan Raichaudhuri (Professor, IIM Calcutta)
    • Dr. Prashant Jha (Consultant, AIIMS, New Delhi and Fellow, Stanfrd BioDesign Lab)
  • Day 6 (March 7, 2014) Workshop on Career Planning on topic "Working in Unconventional Sectors after college" by Ms. Sneha Rao, Member, IIT Mentors.
